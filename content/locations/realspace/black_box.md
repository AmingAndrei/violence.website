The Black Box is a high-security containment and detention complex located beneath the surface of the Chromatican City, serving as both a correctional institution for convicted inmates and a classified holding facility for BLACKSPACE-origin entities and objects.

Architecture

The Black Box is a subterranean cylindrical facility, descending vertically. Its only point of access is through a heavily fortified surface building, situated near the Citadel. This entrance compound is minimal in surface footprint but contains all initial clearance and verification systems. Access to the lower levels is facilitated via a rotating bridge lift that descends through the central shaft, enabling logistical transport and personnel movement between containment tiers.

The internal architecture is composed entirely of shifted-Blackstone, making it capable of self-repair at accelerated rates by intercepting extremely high voltages through embedded cablework, also constructed of shifted-Blackstone. These features render the facility functionally indestructible and practically inescapable.

Containment Functions

The Black Box serves two primary containment roles:

1. Criminal Detention:  Houses convicted civilians.
2. BLACKSPACE Entity and Object Containment
    - Maintains secure chambers for storing _Nullikaryota_ lifeforms and BLACKSPACE constructs.
    - Containment chambers average 49 m², with at least one wall formed of reinforced transparent polymer for observation.
    - Chambers are entirely sealed and soundproofed, with dedicated air filtration systems separate from the city's main circulation grid.
    - Containment conditions vary based on entity type and threat profile, with custom modifications regularly applied.

Security Protocols

In the event of an internal breach or system failure, the Black Box is equipped with multi-phase lockdown procedures, including total surface isolation via blast doors and self-sealing concrete flood protocol to terminate all internal processes, including biological occupants, in critical threat scenarios. Infrared oversees daily operations, infrastructure maintenance, and internal staffing. Ultraviolet holds advisory and regulatory authority over the classification and deposition of BLACKSPACE specimens.

The White Box

Located at the lowest point of the Black Box, the White Box functions as a power core of the Chromatican City. `It is the original structure around which the rest of the Black Box was constructed`. The White Box is situated directly beneath the cylindrical depth of the Black Box, accessible only through a sealed, restricted corridor, with access strictly limited to the Executive, with all operational authority over the White Box vested solely in the Executive.

The White Box appears as a `white, iridescent dome, measuring approximately 15 meters in diameter at its visible upper section. In actuality, it is a perfect sphere, with its lower half extending deep beneath the chamber floor, fully encompassing the spatial volume of its interior. The structure emits a soft glow, and its surface constantly shifts in hue and reflectivity, resisting all direct visual focus.`

The outer shell of the White Box is considered impenetrable from the outside. `This is achieved through the exploitation of Alex Atramentum’s innate ability to alter reality. Any external attempt at entry is rendered physically or conceptually impossible; the structure becomes unreadable and untouchable to the laws of form and force. However, this defensive mechanism creates a paradoxical vulnerability, that while invulnerable from the outside, the structure is fragile from within.`

The White Box serves as a containment unit and `as an energy source, supplying a 178% of the Chromatican City's power needs, with substantial surplus reserves. It was engineered specifically to hold Alex Atramentum, whose containment directly fuels the White Box's operational energy cost and its energy output.`
