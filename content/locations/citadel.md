The **Citadel** is a monumental, metaphysical structure located at the exact geographic and symbolic centre of the **Chromatican City**. Standing at an approximate height of **7 kilometres**, it is the **largest and most significant metaspatial transition point** between Realspace and Blackspace currently known to exist.

**Structure and Composition**

Externally, the Citadel appears as a towering mass of **jagged, interwoven metals and exotic alloys**, exhibiting a chaotic, **haphazardly-welded appearance** that belies its structural stability. The interior of the Citadel is notoriously **unstable, labyrinthine, and hostile**, with its inner architecture only partially mapped.

**Function and Role**

Functionally, the Citadel is understood to be a **hyper-augmented Monolith**—a gateway-type structure capable of facilitating the passage of matter and energy between Realspace and Blackspace.

As a **dimensional stabilizer**, the Citadel passively regulates local tectonic and atmospheric conditions, providing **seismic shielding**. Additionally, due to established **Monolith Placement Rules**—which prohibit the manifestation of new Monoliths within a radius determined by existing Monolith scale—the Citadel’s presence **prevents the appearance of other Monoliths within a 3,000-kilometre radius**.

**Strategic and Anomalous Considerations**

While the Citadel provides structural and dimensional protection to the city, it is also the **primary existential risk factor** associated with the Chromatican City. In the rare event that a hostile entity successfully navigates the Citadel’s transition corridor into Realspace, it is **statistically likely to be of an extremely high threat level**, given the complexity and resistance of the passage.
