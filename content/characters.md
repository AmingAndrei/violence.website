# THE SUPREME ACTS OF VIOLENCE
---
The Supreme Acts of Violence represent the apex of violence as a concept, the most powerful beings ever seen, able to brutalise and force the logic of the world in their favour. They each embody one of the most grievous acts of violence that can be committed, and thus are virtually immortal, as they do not age and killing one will result in it reviving three days later, somewhere in the FLIPSIDE.
