**Pale Field** refers to a distinct species of grass native to **Blackspace**. It is notable for its striking visual contrast against the ambient environment.

**Morphology and Appearance**

Individual blades of Pale Field grass are uniformly **pure white**, lacking any visible pigmentation. This coloration remains consistent across all observed specimens, regardless of biome variance.

**Habitat and Distribution**

Pale Field grasses are typically found in **broad, level expanses** within Blackspace, especially those covered by **red Blackstone sand**. These regions are often referred to as **Pale Fields** due to the overwhelming presence of the species.
