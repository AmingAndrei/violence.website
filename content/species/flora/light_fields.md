Light Field refers to a distinct species of grass native to the OTHERWHERE. It is notable for its striking visual contrast against the ambient environment.

Morphology

Individual blades of Light Field grass are uniformly pure white, lacking any visible pigmentation, capable of bioluminescence. This colouration remains consistent across all observed specimens, regardless of biome variance.

Habitat

Light Field grasses are typically found in broad, level expanses within the OTHERWHERE, especially those covered by red sand. These regions are often referred to as Light Fields due to the overwhelming presence of the species.
