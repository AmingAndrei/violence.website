<span style="color:#FFDE21">Vexers</Span> are a rare, genetically distinct subspecies of <span style="color:#FFDE21">Valence</Span>, representing a highly specialized evolutionary offshoot, 

Origin

The emergence of a <span style="color:#FFDE21">Vexer</Span> is the result of a rare genetic anomaly occurring during the initial formation of a <span style="color:#FFDE21">Valence</Span>:

- Approximately 1% of all <span style="color:#FFDE21">Valences</Span> possess the required sub-genetic sequence associated with <span style="color:#FFDE21">Vexer</Span> capabilities
- This sub-gene arises spontaneously within individuals

Capabilities

The defining trait of a <span style="color:#FFDE21">Vexer</Span> is its innate ability to synthesize Varium. This biochemical capacity grants them access to Vectors, analogous to those used by <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span>.
