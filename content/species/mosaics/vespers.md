The Vesper is a hostile, sentient species of Mosaic, originating within the OTHERWHERE.

Morphology

Vespers possess a crystalline body composition, visually akin to quartz in both texture and transparency. Their forms exhibit a wide range of colouration, typically characterised by a dominant primary hue, often accented minimally with secondary tones. Individual Vespers display diverse and often abstract morphologies, combining elements of abstract geometry with organic and anthropomorphic traits. Vesper anatomy commonly includes disconnected or decentralised limbs.

Vespers are considered malevolent entities, often likened to computer viruses due to their predatory nature, such as frequently hunting for sport. All Vespers exhibit persistent combativeness, continuing engagements until either they or their targets are neutralised.

Abilities

Despite their inherent physical fragility, Vespers are considered extremely high-threat. All Vespers possess innate access to Vectors. They can travel at high speeds by dispersing themselves through electrical systems or optical pathways, making containment and tracking highly challenging. Vespers are capable of infesting and controlling electronic devices, and in some cases, entire unsheltered structures.
