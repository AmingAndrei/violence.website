The <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vesper</Span> is a hostile, sentient species of Mosaic, originating within BLACKSPACE. Recognized for their malevolent behaviour and advanced interfacing capabilities, <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> are among the most dangerous known Voxel-based entities.

Morphology

<span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> possess a crystalline body composition, visually akin to quartz in both texture and transparency. Their forms exhibit a wide range of colouration, typically characterized by a dominant primary hue, often accented minimally with secondary tones. Individual <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> display diverse and often abstract morphologies, combining elements of abstract geometry with organic and anthropomorphic traits, frequently invoking imagery associated with angelic, demonic, or other mythologically malevolent figures. <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vesper</Span> anatomy commonly includes disconnected or decentralized limbs.

<span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> are considered malevolent entities, often likened to computer viruses due to their predatory nature, such as frequently hunting for sport. All <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> exhibit persistent combativeness, continuing engagements until either they or their targets are neutralized.

Abilities

Despite their inherent physical fragility, <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> are considered extremely high-threat due to a combination of abilities:

- All <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> possess innate access to Vectors.
- <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> can travel at high speeds by dispersing themselves through electrical systems, optical pathways, or data-transmission mediums, making containment and tracking highly challenging.
- <span style="color:#FD3DB5">Vespers</Span> are capable of infesting and controlling electronic devices, and in some cases, entire unsheltered structures.
