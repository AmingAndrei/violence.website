`Angels are a classification of advanced mechanical pseudo-species native to BLACKSPACE.`

`Morphology`

`Angels are entirely mechanical in construction. Superficially, their components appear basic, often described as clockwork or crude machinery, devoid of any electronic systems or digital processing elements. However, closer analysis is impossible, as their internal mechanisms resist disassembly and remain incomprehensible beyond surface-level observation. Each Angel is constructed from shifted-Blackstone.`

`Capabilities`

`Angels demonstrate intelligence equal to or exceeding that of humans, with the capacity for abstract reasoning, language, strategy, and independent will. Physically, they are extremely durable, capable of withstanding catastrophic damage, and possess the ability to regenerate their forms.`

`Reality Alteration`

`Every Angel appears to be associated with a singular abstract concept or idea. Their form and capabilities reflect and amplify this conceptual identity, allowing them to manipulate reality in specific ways by nullifying local Coherence, permitting the Angel to enact physical or existential changes that would otherwise be impossible. Their regenerative ability is theorized to stem from this mechanism, rewriting physical damage or structural loss.`

`Fleshcraft`

`Angels are known to create Fleshcraft, organic entities which vary widely in appearance, intelligence, and autonomy, with some being fully sentient, and others being mindless constructs, acting as extensions of the Angel’s will. Fleshcraft may be created singly or in groups, depending on the Angel’s nature and conceptual domain. All Fleshcraft remain loyal to their creator, although degrees of behavioural independence vary.`

`Origin`

`The origin of Angels remains entirely unknown. They do not appear to be manufactured by any known civilization, and their construction does not match any known technological paradigm. While mechanical, they do not fit within the parameters of artificial intelligence or automata.`
