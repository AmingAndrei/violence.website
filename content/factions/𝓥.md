`The Supreme Acts of Violence. Violence Against Art. Against Nature. Against God.`

`For all these years, I've been working on ways of making sure that they cannot ever threaten humanity ever again.`

`Nature is the easiest one to deal with. She showed the least intent of threatening us, but her kin has a different perspective on this. And I'd doubt that her attitude would remain the same if she were to find out what we did to her father.`

`Art is significantly worse. He knows everything there is to know about the city, about our discoveries, about us, about me. I stay awake at night, asking myself: how could I be such an idiot? He is evil, but I was the one enabling him. If there is someone to blame for everything that happened, it's me.`

`For Violence Against God, we managed to trap him below the city. IF he is ever to escape, I doubt we will ever be able to capture him again.`

`I wish there had been an easier way. Just kill them and be done with this. They are mortal; we have proof of at least one of their deaths. But they'll just rise back three days later, and they know that very well.`
