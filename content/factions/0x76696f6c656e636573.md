`The Supreme Acts of Violence are a triumvirate of ultra-powerful entities residing within the OTHERWHERE, widely recognized as its sovereign rulers and the principal source of its existential hostility.`

`Constituent Entities`

`The term "Supreme Acts of Violence" refers collectively to three distinct and autonomous beings, each representing and embodying an archetypal act of ultimate violence. These are:`
- `Violence Against God`
- `Violence Against Art`
- `Violence Against Nature`

`Authority`

`The Acts are the uncontested sovereigns of the OTHERWHERE, occupying positions of absolute dominance. Their authority is not derived from governance or social construct, but from pure alignment with the dimension’s core essence.`

`Immortality`

`One of the most defining and dangerous attributes of the Acts is their functional immortality. Though they can be physically defeated or destroyed, a feat considered nearly impossible due to their overwhelming power, true death remains unattainable. Upon death or dismemberment, remains immediately degrade into red sand. This prevents any forensic or material study.`

`After a 72-hour cycle following confirmed death, the entity will fully reconstitute within the OTHERWHERE, retaining all prior abilities and consciousness. From their perspective, only a few seconds will have passed. This regenerative phenomenon is believed to be the result of their embodied conceptual nature, as each entity IS the act it represents. As such, they are inseparably tied to the dimension’s integrity.`
