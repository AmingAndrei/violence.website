Rubrum is an operational department within the governing structure of the Pale, tasked with internal surveillance, civic enforcement, and the maintenance of containment systems.

Core Responsibilities

1. Citywide Surveillance
    - Rubrum continuously monitors the status, movement, and integrity of the Chromatican City, deploying both human agents and automated systems.
    - Reports generated by Rubrum provide real-time situational awareness to the Executive and department heads.

2. Civic Law Enforcement
    - As the city’s primary policing body, Rubrum agents respond to standard incidents such as civil unrest, criminal activity and interpersonal disputes.
    - Agents are trained in non-lethal conflict resolution, though authorised to escalate under directive.
    - Incidents involving high-risk threats are escalated to Ultraviolet.

Organisational Hierarchy

Rubrum is directed by a single Administrator, appointed internally and confirmed by the Executive. The Administrator reports directly to the Executive.
