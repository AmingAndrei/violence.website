**Yellowcake** is an operational department within the governing structure of the **Pale**, tasked with  the oversight, production, regulation, and distribution of **energy and material resources** across **Chromatican City**, as well as the **management and containment of waste by-products**.

**Core Responsibilities**

1. **Energy Production and Grid Maintenance**
    - Yellowcake oversees the city's **entire electrical grid**, including power generation, transmission infrastructure, load balancing and blackout prevention.
    - Energy is **consolidated, regulated, and redirected** as needed to accommodate both civilian and departmental demands.

2. **Resource Management and Distribution**
    - Controls the **flow of materials** entering and exiting the city, ensuring that **essential goods** are accounted for and equitably distributed, and that **exotic materials** are secured and routed according to protocol.
    - Maintains strict registries and traceability of all critical resources.

3. **Waste Disposal and Repurposing**
    - Yellowcake handles the **collection, neutralization, and repurposing of waste**, including civilian waste streams, industrial runoff and biohazardous by-products.

**Organizational Hierarchy**

Yellowcake is directed by a single **Administrator**, appointed internally and confirmed by the Executive. The Administrator reports directly to the Executive.
