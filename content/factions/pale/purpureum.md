Purpureum is an operational department within the governing structure of the Pale, tasked with the management of public health and the treatment of disease within the Chromatican City.

Core Responsibilities

1. Disease Monitoring
    - Surveillance of public health conditions within the city, including the identification, tracking, and containment of infectious and non-infectious diseases.

2. Medical Treatment
    - Provision and regulation of medical care and the management of healthcare facilities.

Organisational Hierarchy

Purpureum is directed by a single Administrator, appointed internally and confirmed by the Executive. The Administrator reports directly to the Executive.
