Blueprint is an operational department within the governing structure of the Pale, tasked with the design, production, maintenance, and regulation of all infrastructure and fabricated materials within the Chromatican City.

Core Responsibilities

1. Manufacturing Oversight
    - Operates and oversees all manufacturing facilities, encompassing the production of constructs, machinery, components, and structural elements.

2. Infrastructure Construction
    - Responsible for the construction, retrofitting, and controlled deconstruction of buildings, roadways and transit systems.
    - While direct construction efforts have largely diminished due to the resilient, self-sustaining nature of the pre-established Blackstone-based architecture, Blueprint remains on standby for new projects, emergency construction, or structural failure events.
    - Conventional maintenance is minimal for Blackstone structures; however, buildings fabricated by Blueprint using standard composite materials require periodic upkeep and manual recalibration.

3. Interface-Based Structural Repair
    - Facilitates plug-and-repair protocols for Blackstone structures, utilizing embedded conduit systems that allow repairs to be carried out through simple energy injection via tethered interfaces.

Organizational Hierarchy

Blueprint is directed by a single Administrator, appointed internally and confirmed by the Executive. The Administrator reports directly to the Executive.
