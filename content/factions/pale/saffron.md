Saffron is an operational department within the governing structure of the Pale, tasked with  the oversight, production, regulation, and distribution of energy and material resources across the Chromatican City, as well as the management and containment of waste by-products.

Core Responsibilities

1. Energy Production
    - Saffron oversees the city's entire electrical grid, including power generation, transmission infrastructure, load balancing and blackout prevention.
    - Energy is consolidated, regulated, and redirected as needed to accommodate both civilian and departmental demands.

2. Resource Management
    - Controls the flow of materials entering and exiting the city, ensuring that essential goods are accounted for and equitably distributed, and that exotic materials are secured and routed according to protocol, under the supervision of Ultraviolet.
    - Maintains strict registries and traceability of all critical resources.

3. Waste Disposal
    - Saffron handles the collection, neutralization, and repurposing of waste, including civilian waste streams, industrial runoff and biohazardous by-products.

Organizational Hierarchy

Saffron is directed by a single Administrator, appointed internally and confirmed by the Executive. The Administrator reports directly to the Executive.
