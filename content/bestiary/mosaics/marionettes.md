Marionettes (_Mosaica ‗‗‗‗‗‗ marionette_) are all Mosaic species that have been forcefully reverted to default settings and under control of the King in Cyan.
# .anatomy
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Marionettes retain the anatomy and traits they possessed before becoming Marionettes. The only difference between before states is the colouration, with Marionette cells being a pale cyan.
# .behaviour
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Marionettes possess a very limited individuality, and little to no free will. They are completely under the King in Cyan's control, and will follow orders without question.
# .abilities
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Marionettes retain all the abilities they previously possessed.
# .reproduction
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Marionettes are created directly by the King in Cyan by forcefully reverting Mosaics to their base settings. The process is continuous and complicated, and cannot be done remotely.
