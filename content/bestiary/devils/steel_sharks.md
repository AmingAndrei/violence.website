Steel Sharks (_Diavolos diavolos_, <ruby>金属鮫<rt>スティール・シャーク</rt></ruby>) are a the most dangerous and violent species of Devil, the kin of Nature, red in tooth and claw.
# .anatomy
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Steel Sharks are extremely humanoid in appearance, standing at ~2 m tall. Their bodies are made out numerous metallic plates and mechanical parts which are capable or rearranging themselves on a whim. Their entire cellular structure is made of metal. Within their chests is located an engine, which acts as their 'heart'. Their faces display the entire range of human emotions and expressions, even their eyes appear to react to stimuli, although they're purely cosmetic. Most if not all parts of their body are extremely sharp, such as their serrated teeth or chainsaw-like hair strands. Steel Sharks are unanimously agreed to be the apex of the beauty standard no matter the orientation, species, or personal conception about them.
# .behaviour
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Steel Sharks sapient, voluntarily aggressive and find pleasure in causing as much violence as possible. They hunt exclusively for sport. Steel Sharks are the most Anima-dependent Devil species. If they lack the large enough Anima necessary, only found within the FLIPSIDE, they will eventually collapse into a low energy state, vaguely resembling large weaponry, although they remain conscious during this state. While they do display sexual dimorphism similar to humans, they have no inherent concept of gender or sex.
# .abilities
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

The rule of thumb is that there is nothing other Devils can do that Steel Sharks can't also do, if not better.

Steel Sharks have extremely developed senses, capable of pinpoint precision echolocation, thermal imaging, chemoreception, and airwave sensitivity. This makes them near omniscient of their surroundings up to 100 m. This, combined with their extreme speed, intelligence, and strength, makes a single Steel Shark a one man army. And while they're normally loud and explosive, they can choose to become completely silent, which combined with their dexterity and speed, makes them impossible to track.

Steel Sharks are absurdly durable. The metal their cells are composed off is harder than tungsten carbide, have a melting point of ~5000°C, are self-sharpening, self-healing, are completely chemically inert, block radiation, and all while having a density similar to silver. The only way to kill one is to damage its engine. All other types of damage will not affect their senses and only partially slow them down, with a single engine rev being capable of regenerating one third of their body mass. Damage to the engine will redirect all healing towards the engine, and if severe enough, will either require to Anima density of Pandiavolum to heal, or kill the Steel Shark.

Steel Sharks can absorb medium to small objects and devices. Once they make direct contact with said device or object, the cells and mechanical structure of the Steel Shark will disassemble it into its smallest possible components, storing them in its body, These components can then be rebuilt on command, recreating the object perfectly. This extends to all sort of trinkets, and they can even combine some of the stored objects into different once during reassembly, which they can effortlessly split back again.

Steel Sharks are able to interact with radio waves. They can both hear them, understanding whatever is transmitted through the frequency, and respond. When speaking, Steel Sharks emit both audible sound and radio waves, and they cannot stop and just speak through one or the other. In their low energy state, they can only communicate through radio, along with clanking. They can intercept all frequencies at once, and can choose to speak at any frequency or at every frequency at once.

To combat their obvious weakness of requiring a large Anima density to do anything, Steel Sharks can consume blood as fuel. This also comes with a downside, as the Steel Shark be on a timer until the fuel runs out, and actions such as revving or using their maximum strength will drain it much faster. This also means that the Steel Shark can remain indefinitely active outside the FLIPSIDE if it has enough blood.

Steel Sharks are not omnipotent, as they fall into several categories, determining their general abilities, fighting style, and to a slight degree their appearance:

# .reproduction
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Steel Sharks do not reproduce. Individuals spontaneously manifests within Pandiavolum.
# .diet
<div class="bestiary-divider"></div>

Steel Sharks do not eat, or have any real drive to do so. Their only real requirement and need is blood in order to stay active in low Anima environments, which they consume through cellular absorption, meaning as long as it makes contact with the body, the blood will be absorbed and used as fuel. All of their cells also act as taste receptors. They also have a preference towards 'traditional' fuels, such as gas, although this does nothing for them.
