**Acheron** was a clandestine, multidisciplinary research programme headquartered in the **Carpathian Mountains**, formed by a coalition of **nine private research entities**. Active during the early BLACKSPACE exploration era, Acheron was responsible for a range of pioneering scientific breakthroughs, most notably the **discovery and initial exploration of BLACKSPACE**.

**Formation and Purpose**

Established under classified parameters, Acheron's foundational goal was the **investigation of anomalous phenomena** detected in the Carpathian region. The programme integrated multiple disciplines, and through a cooperative research framework, aimed to map and extract usable knowledge.

**Discovery of BLACKSPACE**

On ██-██-████, Acheron successfully made **first contact with BLACKSPACE**, an act that catalysed an era of radical scientific discovery. All findings were **immediately classified**, with dissemination withheld from **both local and international authorities**. The existence of BLACKSPACE, as well as the discoveries associated with it, remained a **closely guarded secret** within the confines of the Acheron structure.

**The Contact and the Ashen Winter Incident**

On ██-██-████, Acheron, under the direction of `Mihaela Rebreanu`, initiated direct contact with an entity referred to as `the Maniac`. Although initially described as cooperative and instrumental in the expansion of BLACKSPACE-related knowledge, this contact is now regarded as a **critical misstep**. Subsequent events, directly attributed to this interaction, culminated in the **Ashen Winter Incident**. The incident resulted in the **permanent deactivation of Acheron**.

**Legacy**

Despite its eventual downfall, the **data infrastructure and catalogued research of Acheron** remain of critical importance. Its archives and surviving systems now serve as foundational assets for both the **Pale** and the **Chromatican City** as a whole.
