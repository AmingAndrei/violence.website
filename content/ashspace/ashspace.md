ASHSPACE is the designated term for the primary dimension comprising observable and measurable reality. ASHSPACE encompasses all known entities, phenomena, and locations recognized as naturally occurring and physically consistent.

Post-Ashen Winter Incident

Following the events of the Ashen Winter Incident, ASHSPACE contemporary theoretical models suggest that ASHSPACE terminates or becomes undefined at a radial boundary ~30,000,000 km from Earth's surface, beyond which no matter or phenomena are observed.
